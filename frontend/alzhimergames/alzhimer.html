The Healing Journey: Engaging Through Play

Tile Tussle

Improves numerical skills and strategic thinking.

Play Description

Rules
 A brain-bending battle against scrambled squares where your wits are your weapon!Plan your moves strategically. Undo moves if needed. Take breaks when stuck. Shuffle the board if necessary.
Choose Difficulty – Select Easy (3x3), Medium (4x4), or Hard (5x5); the board adjusts accordingly.
Move Tiles – Click and drag a tile to swap it with the empty space.
Win the Game – Arrange all tiles in the correct order to complete the puzzle.

Sudoko

Enhances logical reasoning and numerical skills.

Play Description

Rules
 This addictive number game is all about logic, strategy, and a touch of patience. Fill the grid so that every row, column, and 3x3 box contains the numbers 1 to 9 without repeating.
Starting Numbers: Some numbers are already filled in as clues.
No Repeats: Each row, column, and 3x3 box must contain all numbers 1 to 9 without duplicates.
Perfect for sharpening your mind and relaxing your soul.

Solitaire

Promotes planning, strategy, patience, and focus.

Play Description

Rules
 the perfect game to unwind or sharpen your mind! Arrange cards in descending order, alternate colors, and build foundation piles to win.
Build down stack cards in descending order (King to Ace) and alternate colors.
Start with Aces and build up to Kings by suit.
Flip cards one at a time (or three, depending on the version) to find useful cards.
Fill an empty pile with a King or a stack starting with a King.
Complete all four foundation piles to win the game!

Connect 4

Develops pattern recognition and strategic thinking.

Play Description

Rules
 Two players take turns placing colored discs into a vertical grid. The goal is to connect four of your own discs horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Players must think strategically to both create their own lines of four and block their opponent from doing the same.

Crossword

Maintains vocabulary, language skills, and memory recall.

Play Description

Rules
 Players are presented with a grid of white squares intersected by black squares.
You can choose your mode normal or hard.
The goal is to fill in the white squares with letters, forming words or phrases, based on clues provided.
Clues are typically definitions or wordplay.

Simon Says

Improves visual processing and motor skills.

Play Description

Rules
 A sequence of four colors is displayed, each blinking a certain number of times in a seemingly random pattern.
After the sequence finishes, the player must reproduce the exact blinking pattern by clicking on the corresponding colors the same number of times they blinked in the original sequence.
The game increases in difficulty as the player progresses, likely by increasing the length of the sequence, the number of colors, the speed of the blinking, or a combination of these factors.

Rules
 Catch falling emojis by clicking as they appear.

2048

Improves numerical skills and strategic thinking.

Play Description

Rules
 Combine tiles of the same number in an attempt to create a tile with the value 2048.
Pressing the arrow keys or swiping across the game board will move all tiles in the direction of the key pressed/swipe.
After all the tiles are done moving a new tile with the value of 2 or 4 will be created in a random empty cell.
Two tiles with the same number will merge when they collide to create a single tile with double the value.
The game is over when there are no valid ways for you to move the tiles.

Rules
 Use your right and left keys to move the paddle to bounce the ball up and break the blocks.
If you miss the ball, you score drops to zero and the blocks will reset

Minesweeper

Exercises logical reasoning and spatial reasoning.

Play Description

Rules
 Before starting Minesweeper, you select a difficulty level: Easy (9x9 grid with 10 mines), Medium (16x16 grid with 40 mines), or Hard (30x30 grid with 160 mines).
Clicking anywhere on the board starts the game and the timer.
Numbers displayed on revealed cells indicate the number of mines in the adjacent cells.
To mark a cell you suspect contains a mine, use Shift + Click to place a flag.
The goal is to uncover all safe cells (those without mines).
Clicking on a mine results in a game over.
You win by successfully revealing all the safe cells without detonating any mines.

Matching Shape

Improves visual perception, spatial reasoning, and fine motor skills.

Play Description

Rules
 Drag and drop the colored shapes into the matching black shapes on the grid.
Use the left and right arrow keys to rotate the shapes.
For every correctly placed shape, your score will increase out of 7. The score is displayed in the top right corner.
You have 2 minutes to complete the puzzle. The timer is displayed at the top center. When the time runs out, the correct solution will be shown.
There are 2 modes: Begineer and Legend.
Successfully match all colored shapes to the black shapes before time runs out!
