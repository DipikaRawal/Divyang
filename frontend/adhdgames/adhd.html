ADHD Focus Games

Engaging games designed to enhance focus, memory, and cognitive skills for individuals with ADHD

Speed Typing

Improves focus, processing speed, hand-eye coordination.

Play Description

Rules
 Type the given word as quickly and accurately as possible.
Words appear on screen, and you must type them exactly.
Each word has a time limit (e.g., 10 seconds).
Points for accuracy.No penalty for mistakes.
3 game modes:Easy, medium and Hard.
The game ends when the timer runs out or all challenges are completed.
Final score is displayed.

BreakOut Room

Challenge reflexes and focus for sharpening hand-eye coordination, also attention skills.

Play Description

Rules
 Use your right and left keys to move the paddle to bounce the ball up and break the blocks.
If you miss the ball, you score drops to zero and the blocks will reset

Flappy Bird

Improves focus, processing speed, hand-eye coordination.

Play Description

Rules
 The player controls a bird that continuously falls and must tap to make it flap and stay in the air.
The objective is to navigate through narrow gaps between pipes without hitting them.
The game requires precise timing, coordination, and patience to achieve high scores.

Color Paths

Enhances attention span, reaction time, hand-eye coordination.

Play Description

Rules
 Tap/ Press the spacebar to make the bird fly and navigate through pipes without hitting them.
Each successful pass through pipes earns points. The game becomes more challenging as the bird’s speed increases.
Stay alert to avoid obstacles at all costs.
The game ends when you crash into an obstacle.
Try to beat your previous high score.

Ping Pong

Sharpens reflexes, hand-eye coordination, focus.

Play Description

Rules
 Use the paddle (↑ and ↓) to bounce the ball back and forth.
Your goal is to prevent the ball from falling off your side while trying to get it past your opponent's paddle to score points.
The game can be played with one AI opponent.
The game has no time limit or a point goal, and the objective is to keep the ball in play.
React quickly and focus on defending your side. The faster your reactions, the higher your score will be.

Tilting Maze

Improves fine motor skills, spatial reasoning, concentration.

Play Description

Rules
 Guide the ball through a maze using the joystick, merging four colored dots into one as you navigate the path.
Once the dots are merged, guide the ball to the hole in the center of the maze to complete the game.
The maze features various obstacles that require careful movement to avoid.
Press H for hard mode and E for easy mode. In hard mode, you need to avoid black holes.
If a ball falls into the hole, press the spacebar to reset the game.
The goal is to complete the game as quickly and accurately as possible. A steady hand and good spatial awareness are essential.

Whake A Mole

Boosts reaction time, hand-eye coordination.

Play Description

Rules
 Moles randomly pop up from holes, and you need to hit(click) them with a mallet before they disappear.
Each successful hit earns points. You have a limited amount of time to hit as many moles as possible.
Avoid hitting any other objects or missing moles.
The game speeds up as you progress.
The highest score is earned by quick reflexes.

Crossy Road

Challenge reflexes and focus for sharpening hand-eye coordination, also attention skills.

Play Description

Rules
 Guide your character across roads by avoiding traffic and obstacles.
Tap the keys or use arrow keys to move and avoid being hit by cars .
Each level presents more challenging traffic patterns.
The game ends if you’re hit by a vehicle.
The goal is to travel as far as possible.
High scores are based on distance covered.

Snake and Ladder

Helps with number recognition, turn-taking, strategic thinking, patience.

Play Description

Rules
 Choose the number of players and the color you want.
Roll a dice and move along the board, landing on numbered squares.
If you land on a snake, you slide backward; if you land on a ladder, you climb upward.
The first player to reach the final square wins.
Take turns rolling the dice and strategizing your moves.
It’s a simple yet fun game that depends on luck and strategy.
Keep track of your progress and stay patient.

Memory Card

Improves working memory, visual recognition, concentration.

Play Description

Rules
 Flip over two cards at a time to try and match pairs.
The goal is to remember the positions of the cards to make successful matches.
Each correct match scores points.
The game ends when all pairs have been matched.
The fewer moves you take, the higher your score.

Insect Catcher

Enhances reaction time, hand-eye coordination, visual tracking.

Play Description

Rules
Select your Favourite Insect.
Insects fly around the screen, and your task is to catch them by tapping on them.
Each insect caught earns points.
The game becomes more difficult as insects move faster.
Avoid missing any insects to maximize your score.
Keep playing to improve your reaction time

Archery

improves working memory, visual recognition, and concentration

Play Description

Rules
 The player controls a virtual bow and arrow, aiming to hit a target with precision.
The gameplay is simple—click or tap to stretch the bowstring, adjust the aim, and release to shoot the arrow.
Accuracy is key, as hitting the center (bullseye) scores the most points, while outer rings give fewer points. The goal is to test the player’s focus, timing, and hand-eye coordination.
