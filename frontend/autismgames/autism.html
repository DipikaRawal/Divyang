Stimulating Games For Autism

Inclusive and engaging games designed to be accessible and beneficial for individuals with autism spectrum disorder, promoting focus, communication, and cognitive growth.

Tile Tussle

Improves numerical skills and strategic thinking.

Play Description

Rules
 A brain-bending battle against scrambled squares where your wits are your weapon!Plan your moves strategically. Undo moves if needed. Take breaks when stuck. Shuffle the board if necessary.
Choose Difficulty – Select Easy (3x3), Medium (4x4), or Hard (5x5); the board adjusts accordingly.
Move Tiles – Click and drag a tile to swap it with the empty space.
Win the Game – Arrange all tiles in the correct order to complete the puzzle.

Colour Puzzles

Enhances pattern recognition, color perception, and cognitive flexibility in a fun way.

Play Description

Rules
 It a puzzle-based strategy game that requires a mix of skill and luck.
The objective is to change the color of the squares until the entire screen is a single solid color.
Starting with the square in the upper left corner, you can change the color of the blocks in order to match that of the surrounding squares.
This is done repeatedly until the entire screen is one color.
You must do so in under 22 moves to win!
The game is controlled by the color buttons at the bottom of the screen.

Tetris

Develops spatial reasoning, problem-solving, and organization skills in a structured manner.

Play Description

Rules
 The player must strategically position falling blocks to create complete horizontal lines.
When a line is filled, it disappears and score increases, and the blocks above shift down. The game speeds up as the player clears more lines, increasing the challenge.
The goal is to clear as many lines as possible before the blocks reach the top of the screen, ending the game.
Press esc to pause the game.

Tilting Maze

Enhances balance, coordination, and motor planning, beneficial for sensory integration.

Play Description

Rules
 Guide the ball through a maze using the joystick, merging four colored dots into one as you navigate the path.
Once the dots are merged, guide the ball to the hole in the center of the maze to complete the game.
The maze features various obstacles that require careful movement to avoid.
Press H for hard mode and E for easy mode. In hard mode, you need to avoid black holes.
If a ball falls into the hole, press the spacebar to reset the game.
The goal is to complete the game as quickly and accurately as possible. A steady hand and good spatial awareness are essential.

Starfall

Enhances visual tracking, reflexes, and coordination with engaging falling object mechanics.

Play Description

Rules
 The goal is to catch falling stars while avoiding obstacles.
Players must move quickly and position themselves correctly to collect as many stars as possible before time runs out.
The game enhances reflexes, coordination, and visual tracking.

Simon Says

IImproves memory, attention, and ability to follow instructions, essential for daily tasks.

Play Description

Rules
 A sequence of four colors is displayed, each blinking a certain number of times in a seemingly random pattern.
After the sequence finishes, the player must reproduce the exact blinking pattern by clicking on the corresponding colors the same number of times they blinked in the original sequence.
The game increases in difficulty as the player progresses, likely by increasing the length of the sequence, the number of colors, the speed of the blinking, or a combination of these factors.

Scrabble

Strengthens vocabulary, spelling, and communication skills .

Play Description

Rules
 Strategically place letter tiles on the board to create high-scoring words while blocking your opponents. Scrabble is a fantastic way to expand your vocabulary, enhance your spelling, and enjoy some friendly competition.
Words must be valid and connect to existing ones.
Connect words like a crossword to score points!

Cross road

Develops planning skills, quick decision-making, and spatial awareness.

Play Description

Rules
 Guide your character across roads by avoiding traffic and obstacles.
Tap the keys or use arrow keys to move and avoid being hit by cars .
Each level presents more challenging traffic patterns.
The game ends if you’re hit by a vehicle.
The goal is to travel as far as possible.
High scores are based on distance covered.

Breakout Room

Challenge reflexes and focus for sharpening hand-eye coordination, also attention skills.

Play Description

Rules
 Use your right and left keys to move the paddle to bounce the ball up and break the blocks.
If you miss the ball, you score drops to zero and the blocks will reset

Bullet hell

Improves reaction time, hand-eye coordination, and focus by navigating fast-paced challenges.

Play Description

Rules
 Here speed, precision, and quick reflexes are your best allies.
Your goal is to survive an onslaught of enemy bullets while taking down as many foes as possible to boost your score, starting at 0 with a 1x multiplier.
Use the Arrow keys or WASD to maneuver through the chaos, and press Z or B to fire at enemies.
When the screen becomes overwhelming, slow down time with X or N to carefully navigate, or unleash a screen-clearing bomb with C or M to reset the battlefield.
If you need a moment to strategize, hit P to pause the action.
The longer you survive and the more enemies you destroy, the higher your score will climb.
Can you dodge the relentless bullet storm, outsmart your enemies, and rise to the top?
The challenge is yours—good luck!

Word Finder

Enhances visual scanning and word recognition.

Play Description

Rules
 This exciting game challenges you to uncover hidden words in a grid of letters. Perfect for sharpening your vocabulary and pattern recognition skills. Find hidden words in a letter grid by connecting adjacent letters (up, down, sideways, or diagonally). Words must be 3+ letters. No proper nouns or repeats in one word. Race to find the most words before time runs out!

Flappy Bird

Boosts motor control, timing, and perseverance with repetitive but engaging gameplay.

Play Description

Rules
 The player controls a bird that continuously falls and must tap to make it flap and stay in the air.
The objective is to navigate through narrow gaps between pipes without hitting them.
The game requires precise timing, coordination, and patience to achieve high scores.
