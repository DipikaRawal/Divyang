Chess

Enhances planning and sequential thinking.

Play Description

Rules
 Chess is a strategic two-player game played on an 8x8 grid.
Each player starts with 16 pieces: one king, one queen, two rooks, two bishops, two knights, and eight pawns.
The goal is to checkmate the opponent's king, meaning the king is in a position to be captured ("in check") and cannot escape. Pieces move in unique ways: pawns move forward but capture diagonally, knights move in an L-shape, bishops diagonally, rooks horizontally or vertically, the queen combines rook and bishop moves, and the king moves one square in any direction.
Special moves include castling (king and rook swap for safety), en passant (pawn capture), and pawn promotion (reaching the opponent's back rank).
The game ends in checkmate, stalemate (no legal moves), or a draw by agreement, repetition, or the 50-move rule.
You can choose whether you want to plat with AI agent or other friend and your color.

Bullet hell

Improves visual tracking and attention.

Play Description

Rules
Here speed, precision, and quick reflexes are your best allies.
Your goal is to survive an onslaught of enemy bullets while taking down as many foes as possible to boost your score, starting at 0 with a 1x multiplier.
Use the Arrow keys or WASD to maneuver through the chaos, and press Z or B to fire at enemies.

Can you dodge the relentless bullet storm, outsmart your enemies, and rise to the top?

Tower Block

Improves fine motor skills and spatial reasoning.

Play Description

Rules
 Click (or press the spacebar) to place the block.
When the game begins, your task is to stack incoming blocks on top of each other to build the tallest tower possible.
However, there’s a catch: if you place a block slightly outside the edge of the one below it, the overhanging part will be cut off, making the next block shorter.
Precision is key—every move counts as you aim to balance speed and accuracy to create the highest tower without toppling it.
Can you master the art of perfect placement and reach new heights?
Let the stacking begin!

Tetris

Enhances spatial reasoning and visual-motor coordination.

Play Description

Rules
The player must strategically position falling blocks to create complete horizontal lines.
When a line is filled, it disappears and score increases, and the blocks above shift down. The game speeds up as the player clears more lines, increasing the challenge.
The goal is to clear as many lines as possible before the blocks reach the top of the screen, ending the game.
Press esc to pause the game.
